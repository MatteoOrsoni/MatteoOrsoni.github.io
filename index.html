About Me

Hi there. I'm Matteo Orsoni, a researcher at the intersection of psychology, neuroscience, and artificial intelligence.

With a PhD in Psychological Sciences, I'm passionate about using AI and machine learning to enhance our understanding of the mind and improve learning and mental health.

My work spans from developing intelligent educational tools to pioneering new approaches in mental health assessment and treatment. I'm particularly excited about personalizing learning and psychological interventions through technology.

Current Research Focus:

  • Machine learning for cognitive profile analysis
  • Adaptive AI systems for personalized learning
  • Reinforcement learning in educational contexts
  • Advancing psychometric assessment boundaries
  • Eye movement studies in psychiatric conditions

While I'm well-versed in the technical aspects (R, Python, Machine learning, Statistics), my primary focus is on the real-world impact of my research. I've published in international journals (Scopus and WOS indexed), presented at global conferences, and collaborate on exciting projects with researchers worldwide.

Interested in AI's role in understanding and enhancing human cognition? Let's connect! Check out my publications or reach out to learn more about my work.
